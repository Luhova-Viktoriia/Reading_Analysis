# Dashboard 3: Top 30 Books – Genre & Subgenre Curation Analytics

## Overview

This dashboard focuses on curated book selection, analyzing the top 30 books of 2025 through a genre and subgenre classification system.

It highlights how books are evaluated not only individually, but also as part of a structured thematic taxonomy.

## Core Visualization

### Treemap: Genre & Subgenre Mapping

- Top 30 books visualized using a treemap structure
- Interactive highlighting of genres and subgenres
- Books assigned to multiple genres where applicable
- Dominant genre selection based on overall thematic interpretation

## Color Encoding Logic

- Each genre is assigned a unique base color
- Subgenres inherit and refine the color mapping
- Each book is colored according to its dominant genre/theme
- Color represents interpretative classification, not strict data grouping

## Analytical Approach

This dashboard applies a curated classification logic:

- books may belong to multiple genres
- dominant genre is selected based on thematic strength
- visualization reflects subjective + analytical categorization
- emphasis on interpretation rather than strict taxonomy

## Purpose

This dashboard is designed to:
- identify the most valuable books of 2025
- analyze genre distribution among top-rated books
- visualize thematic dominance across reading choices
- support qualitative evaluation of reading preferences

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Treemap visualization
- Highlight interaction layer
- Relational dataset with genre/subgenre mapping logic
