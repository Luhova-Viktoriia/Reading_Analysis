# Dashboard 2: Reading Series Progress & Structure Analytics

## Overview

This dashboard focuses on reading series behavior, tracking progression, completion status, and structural relationships between genres, subgenres, and series.

It provides insight into how reading unfolds across connected book universes rather than individual books.

## Series-Genre Structure Matrix

- Matrix of genres vs reading series (2025)
- Visual mapping of series to genres
- Expandable hierarchy: genre → subgenre → series
- Checkbox-style relationship indicators

## Reading Progress by Series

- Read before 2025 (dark green)
- Read in 2025 (light green)
- Left to read (light red)

## Series Consumption Analysis

- Total books per series
- Books read per series
- Area chart showing total vs read books

## Series Rating Distribution

- Rating ratio (%) across series

## Series Overview Metrics

- Max books in a series
- Average books per series
- Minimum books per series
- Total books in series
- Total read books in series

## Series Completion Metrics

- Completed series count
- Total unique series
- Total books across all series

## Interactive Filtering

- Rating-based slicer for dynamic filtering of series analysis

## Analytical Purpose

This dashboard is designed to:
- Track reading progression across series
- Understand completion behavior
- Analyze genre-to-series relationships
- Evaluate engagement with long-form reading structures

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- PostgreSQL
- Relational data model with bridge tables
- Series-level aggregation logic
